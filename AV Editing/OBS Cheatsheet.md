### Keyboard shortcuts

| Keys | Function |
|------|----------|
|CTRL + # | Set a chapter mark|
|CTRL + P | Pause/Resume Recording|
|CTRL + SHIFT + R | Start/Stop Recording|
|CTRL + SHIFT + P | Hide/Show Preview|
